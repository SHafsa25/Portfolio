Incident Management & Risk Mitigation

Severity-2 Events & High-Stakes Decision Making

$1.5B Potential Seller Losses Prevented (YTD)

Deep expertise in Severity-2 incident management within high-traffic e-commerce environments, where rapid response and precise decision-making are critical to protecting merchants and maintaining marketplace trust.

My focus is on early detection and containment of pricing-driven anomalies—situations where sudden traffic spikes and abnormal order volumes indicate potential pricing or listing errors. In these scenarios, I lead an on-call incident response workflow, coordinating cross-functional teams to assess impact, stabilize the issue, and prevent downstream harm to sellers and customers.

Key Elements of My Approach

Traffic Monitoring

Monitoring traffic and order behavior to identify abnormal demand patterns

Rapid Validation

Quick validation of potential pricing or catalog discrepancies

Coordinated Response

Containing impact including order-level controls where required

Execution to safeguard seller experience and marketplace integrity

Through disciplined incident governance, data-driven assessment, and timely containment actions, I have helped prevent significant financial exposure for sellers during high-risk events. This work reflects my broader strength in operational risk management, escalation leadership, and high-stakes decision-making.
